About This Project

Welcome to the Task Management App! This application is designed to help you efficiently manage your tasks and stay organized. Whether you're juggling multiple projects or just need a better way to keep track of your to-do list, our app has you covered.

This project was developed as the final project for the Angular course organized by Alte University. Over the course of 4 months, we delved deep into Angular and Firebase to bring this app to life. Additionally, we utilized RxJS to manage asynchronous data streams and enhance the app's responsiveness.

Key features of the Task Management App include:

  • Create, update, and delete tasks
  • Organize tasks by categories
  • Set deadlines and reminders
  • Track progress and completion status
  • User-friendly interface with responsive design

We hope this app helps you stay productive and manage your time more effectively. Thank you for using our Task Management App!
